Oven-Roasted Broccoli

Description

The best way to cook broccoli.

Ingredients

  • 8 Ounces broccoli florets
  • 1.5 teaspoons olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • coarse grind black pepper
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Put broccoli in mixing bowl, pour oil over, and mix well.
  3. Add salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste.
  4. Spread broccoli out on baking sheet.
  5. Cook in oven for 20 minutes.
  6. Broccoli is ready to eat immediately.
