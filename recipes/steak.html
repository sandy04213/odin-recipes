Pan-Seared Ribeye Steak

Description

My favorite steak. The fattiest cut. Supposedly the worst for you. Pair with a scotch.

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye steak, about 1 inch thick
  • 1/2 teaspoon olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • coarse grind black pepper
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Place large cast iron skillet on stove at high heat. Give it a couple minutes to get really hot. You want it just shy of smoking.
  3. Pour 1/4 teaspoon on each side of steak, and work it all over steak with your hands to cover evenly.
  4. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste on both sides of steak.
  5. Place steak in skillet. If it doesn't immediately sizzle, the pan was not hot enough.
  6. After two or three minutes, turn to other side.
  7. After other side has cooked for about two minutes, place skillet in the preheated oven, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Remove steak from oven, and place it on plate to rest for 5 minutes. Steak is now cooked to rare perfection and ready to eat.
